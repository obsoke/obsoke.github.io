As a consolation prize, here are some links to cool stuff I found on internets this weekend. » After the news that Rogers would not be upgrading the HTC Dream to a newer version of Android, customers must have complained because MobileSyrup reported that Rogers is offering free upgrades to Dream owners up to a HTC Magic. I don't think I can give up my keyboard though. Plus the screen is tiny enough as it is, a virtual keyboard taking up half of the screen wouldn't be desirable.
